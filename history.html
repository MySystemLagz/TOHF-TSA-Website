    Founders

    Wolfram von Tohf

    Wolfram von Tohf was born August 27, 2014 in Stuttgart, Germany. He was the youngest of two brothers. His dad, Markus von Tohf, was an avid lover of cars. While in college, he saw the many advancements in electric cars and decided to start a company of his own. During his time in college, he met Sarah Weinstein and they fell in love. She too was very well-versed in cars and would later help Wolfram make TOHF.

    Sarah von Tohf

    Sarah von Tohf was born May 19, 2017. She was an only child and lived with just her mother. Her mother was a mechanic and taught Sarah about cars. Sarah believed that a car should look as good as it performs. She wanted to make cars that were amazing but she wanted to stand out. She took an interest in electric cars and headed to college where she met Wolfram.

    History

    TOHF is a German company that makes electric vehicles.

    On January 24, 2046, the company was founded. The company took the name of Wolframâ€™s last name, Tohf.

    Planning for the first car started in 2047. TOHF wanted their first car to be a hit. With other competitors in the electric car industry, they had high expectations to uphold

    The first car, the TFX 1660Ti, was created and displayed to the public on November, 3, 2050. It was critically acclaimed by many and it put TOHF on the spotlight. The company had made a name for itself and now they had the foundation set in place.

    The second car, the TFX 1770Ti, was a car meant for the family. They wanted to make sure they reached every kind of user and so making a car for the family driver was necessary. It was released June 7, 2051. It was very popular, much like the TFX 1660Ti.

    Their latest car, the TFX 1880Ti, was not for the regular user. This one was a showcase of what an electric car is capable of and proving that they are just as good, and even, better than gas cars. It was a supercar and was pretty expensive. When it was released, many were surprised. The car was more expensive than the other cars they had made. Also, despite being an electric car, it performed as well as other supercars such as the Ferrari 488 and the Lamborghini Aventador.
